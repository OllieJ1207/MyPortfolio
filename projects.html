Mum's Scentsy App (Website)

I started this project for my mum towards the end of October 2025. My mum is an Independent Scentsy Consultant, which means that she sells products on behalf of Scentsy, and she asked me if I could develop her a website for her to "record her orders when they come in". I started developing it straight away, and I have successfully managed to get it to a point where she is able to use it! Every now and then, she asks if I can add something to it, which I am always able to do!

The website is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have also used Google Firebase to store all of the data that she inputs into the website, which is great because I'm able see everything on the Firebase console, meaning that I can edit any of the data if I need too.
